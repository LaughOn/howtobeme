How to Take Care of Dogs like Me!

  1. Ask the owners for a time to feed them breakfast and dinner
  2. Go to the house and go to where the dogs are kept
  3. Let the out
  4. Walk with them outside and make sure they use the bathroom
  5. Go back inside and feed them
  6. Make sure they eat everything
  7. Check thier water dish(s)
  8. Play with the dogs
  9. Make sure they havent messed up the house
  10. Either put them in their cage or let them roam around
  11. Leave making sure you locked the house
  12. Go back for dinner
  13. Repeat steps 2-11
  14. Add any additional visits at your own leisure
