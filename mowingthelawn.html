How to Mow the Lawn like Me!

  1. Put on work clothes and athletic shoes
  2. Go into the garage
  3. Lake the lawnmower from the garage to the driveway
  4. Take gas lid off the lawnmower
  5. Get the gas from the bucket beside the fence
  6. Pour the gas in the lawnmower
  7. Put the gas back
  8. Screw the top back on
  9. Make sure the lawnmower is fully choked
  10. Pull the pull string until the lawnmower starts
  11. Activate the mower blades by pushing the lever down
  12. Mow the front yard
  13. Mow the back yard starting with the top terrace
  14. Mow the steep hill
  15. Mow the terraces from top to bottom
  16. Once finished put the mower away
